Free-response:

13. How has the concept of the AU expanded our understanding of the universe?

Answer key:
1. A
2. D
3. A
4. A
5. B

8. B
9. A
10. B
11. The AU is used to calculate the distance between a star and a planet, which is important in determining the habitable zone. If a planet is too close or too far from a star, it may not be suitable for life.
12. Technology allows astronomers to gather data from distant objects in the universe, helping them to explore and discover new worlds beyond our solar system.
13. The concept of the AU has allowed us to comprehend the incredible distances between celestial objects in the universe, expanding our understanding of our place in the universe.
