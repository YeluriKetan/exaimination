10. The law of supply and demand states that as the price of a product increases, the quantity supplied also increases, while the quantity demanded decreases. This relationship is crucial in agricultural economics as it helps producers and consumers make informed decisions about what to produce, at what price, and how much to consume. It also plays a significant role in the allocation of resources and the overall health of the agricultural market.
