Free Response Questions:
12. How does socialization shape our beliefs, attitudes, and behaviors?

Answer Key:

12. Socialization shapes our beliefs, attitudes, and behaviors by teaching us societal norms, values, and behaviors that are expected in different social situations. It also helps us to internalize these societal expectations and make them a part of our own identity.
13. Sociologists use a variety of methods such as surveys, experiments, and observations to study the complexities of human society. They also analyze data and use theories to understand the patterns and relationships within society.
14. Social stratification plays a role in the unequal distribution of resources and opportunities among different groups in society. It can lead to social inequalities and perpetuate systems of oppression. Understanding social stratification is crucial for addressing issues of inequality and working towards a more equitable society.
