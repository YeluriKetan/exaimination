True/False Questions:

A. True
B. False

9. True or False: Planets closer to the sun have longer years.
A. True
B. False

A. True
B. False

Free-Response Questions:
11. How does the distance between two objects affect the strength of gravity between them?

12. Explain the relationship between a planet's distance from the sun and its orbital speed.

Answer Key:
1. C
2. A
3. A
4. A
5. A
6. C
7. D
8. B
9. B
10. A
11. The strength of gravity between two objects decreases as the distance between them increases.
12. The closer a planet is to the sun, the faster it moves in its orbit due to the stronger force of gravity. This results in shorter orbital periods, or years, for these planets.
